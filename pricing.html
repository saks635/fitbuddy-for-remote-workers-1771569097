Frequently Asked Questions

What is FitBuddy?

FitBuddy is an AI-powered virtual fitness coach designed specifically for remote workers. It helps you track workouts, nutrition, and mental health, providing personalized plans and motivation.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro or Premium subscription at any time through your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for the Pro plan?

Yes, the Starter plan acts as a 7-day free trial, giving you a taste of FitBuddy's core features before committing to a paid plan.

How does the AI personalize my plans?

Our AI analyzes your fitness level, goals, available equipment, schedule, and feedback to create dynamic, adaptive training and wellness plans that evolve with you.

# FitBuddy for Remote Workers

## Your AI-Powered Wellness Partner for Remote Work

FitBuddy is an AI-powered virtual fitness coach designed specifically for remote employees aged 22-45 who are tech-savvy and interested in achieving holistic wellness. This multi-page static website showcases FitBuddy's features, mission, pricing, and contact information, providing a clean, modern, and conversion-focused user experience.

## Problem Solved

Remote workers often struggle with maintaining physical and mental well-being due to sedentary lifestyles, lack of routine, and increased stress. Existing solutions often don't cater to their specific needs, leading to decreased health and productivity.

## Our Solution

FitBuddy offers a comprehensive approach to wellness by tracking daily workouts, nutrition, and mental health check-ins. It provides personalized training plans, AI-guided meditation sessions, and incorporates gamification elements to motivate users. By leveraging AI, FitBuddy creates an engaging, adaptive, and supportive environment that encourages remote workers to prioritize their health.

## Key Features

*   **Personalized AI Coaching:** Dynamic workout plans that adapt to your progress, goals, and available equipment.
*   **Holistic Wellness Tracking:** Monitor workouts, nutrition intake, and mental health check-ins in one intuitive platform.
*   **AI-Guided Meditation:** Reduce stress and improve focus with personalized meditation sessions.
*   **Gamified Motivation:** Stay engaged with challenges, rewards, and progress tracking.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** Clean, modern design for a seamless experience.
*   **Responsive Design:** Optimized for all devices, from desktop to mobile.

## Technologies Used

*   **HTML5:** For structuring the web content.
*   **CSS3:** For styling and responsive design.
*   **JavaScript:** For interactive elements like the mobile navigation toggle and form feedback.
